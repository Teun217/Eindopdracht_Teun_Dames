Subtractive Synthesis

A very common type of synthesizer is the subtractive synthesizer. Subtractive synthesis basicallyl means that there is a little screamer (the oscillator) it makes a lot of noise. The job of the instrumentalist is to cut down, subtract, from that sound to form a new sound. On this page I am going to show a couple of features regarding this on the Deepmind 12.

Features on the Deepmind

Oscillator 1

Oscillators are the things that make the noise within a synthesizer. You have two types of waves (square and saw waves) and they both sound a little bit different. You can also combine these waves.

Oscillator 2

The deepmind has 2 oscillators that can play over eachother. Oscillator 2 gives you among other things control over pitch and volume.

Noise level

This slider regulates the volume of the noise.
